Versatile Gen IV SMR Design

Our helium-cooled Gen IV SMR (small modular reactor) design minimizes consumption of precious resources, requires no water body, and is scalable to operate on as little as 10kg of HALEU in the form of TRISO pellets to meet various thermal and electrical energy requirements.

Our large SMR design is wholly contained within the footprint of a football field and is remotely operated. Our designs meet and exceed USNRC safety standards. The designs target improved safety, sustainability, efficiency, and cost.

We are one of the few organizations with the experience of building a pilot fuel plant in addition to supplying expertise for large-scale nuclear installations operating today.
